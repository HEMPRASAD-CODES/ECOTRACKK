Welcome to VIT EcoTrack

VIT EcoTrack is committed to fostering a sustainable future by promoting eco-friendly practices and raising awareness within the campus community. Our mission is to lead by example and inspire everyone to embrace sustainability in their daily lives.

Our Goals

  • Reduce carbon footprint through renewable energy initiatives.
  • Promote waste management and recycling programs.
  • Encourage green spaces and biodiversity on campus.
  • Educate the community about the importance of sustainability.
